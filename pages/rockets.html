What is a Rocket?

Rockets are vehicles used to carry payloads into space, powered by engines that generate thrust through high-speed exhaust gases.

Rocket Uses

Rockets are used for space exploration, launching satellites, and carrying astronauts and cargo to space stations and beyond.

ISRO's PSLV

The Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) is one of ISRO's most successful rockets, used for launching satellites into polar orbits.
