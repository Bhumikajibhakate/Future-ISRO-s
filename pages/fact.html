Cool Space Facts About ISRO!

🚀 ISRO’s First Satellite

Did you know? ISRO launched its first satellite, *Aryabhata*, in 1975. It was named after an ancient Indian mathematician and astronomer!

🌕 India on the Moon

India’s Chandrayaan missions made India one of the few countries to explore the Moon! Chandrayaan-2 even found evidence of water on the Moon.

🪐 Mission to Mars

ISRO's *Mangalyaan* mission was a huge success. It made India the first country to reach Mars on its very first try! Isn’t that amazing?

🌍 Helping from Space

ISRO has many satellites that help predict weather, save lives during disasters, and even connect people across the world. Space is super helpful!

🔭 Space for Everyone!

ISRO isn’t just about rockets. They also teach students like you to be curious about space. Maybe one day you’ll work for ISRO too!
