What is a Nebula?

A nebula is a giant cloud of dust and gas in space. Some nebulae form stars, while others are remnants of dead or dying stars.

Types of Nebulae

Nebulae can be classified into three main types: emission nebulae, reflection nebulae, and planetary nebulae.

Famous Nebulae

Some of the most famous nebulae include the Orion Nebula, the Crab Nebula, and the Ring Nebula.
