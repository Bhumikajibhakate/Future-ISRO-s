Red Giant

A stage in a star's life where it expands and cools after exhausting its hydrogen fuel.

White Dwarf

The remnants of a star that has exhausted its nuclear fuel and shed its outer layers.

Neutron Star

A very dense star formed from the remnants of a supernova explosion, made mostly of neutrons.

Red Giant

Red giants are stars that have consumed the hydrogen in their cores and have begun to burn helium or heavier elements. They are characterized by their large size and reddish color.

White Dwarf

White dwarfs are the final stage in the evolution of stars like our Sun. They are very dense, and their light comes from the residual heat of their former self.

Neutron Star

Neutron stars are incredibly dense remnants left after supernova explosions. They are composed mostly of neutrons and have extremely strong gravitational fields.
